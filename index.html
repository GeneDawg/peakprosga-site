Georgia’s Contractor Platform for Roof Rejuvenation, Maintenance, and Repair

Peak Pros Georgia is the state operational hub for contractors participating in the Peak Pros platform. Approved contractor partners can submit assignments, track opportunities, and work within a structured roof rejuvenation and maintenance system supported by Peak Pros USA.

How the Platform Works

1. Opportunity Identified

Contractors identify a qualifying roof rejuvenation, roof wash, or repair opportunity through their own sales activity, marketing, or approved lead sources.

2. Assignment Submitted

The contractor submits the assignment through the Peak Pros Georgia contractor portal with the homeowner details, scope notes, and project information.

3. Routed Through the Peak Pros System

The platform coordinates assignment routing, territory fulfillment, and billing workflow through Peak Pros USA and the authorized operator structure.

Eligible Service Types

Roof Rejuvenation

Peak 301 roof rejuvenation opportunities routed through the Georgia platform and fulfilled inside the authorized territory structure.

Roof Wash / Maintenance

Contractors can submit roof wash and maintenance-driven opportunities that fit the Peak Pros model.

Roof Repair

Qualifying repair opportunities can be routed through the contractor portal with notes, documentation, and project details.

Contractor Portal

The Peak Pros Georgia contractor portal is designed for approved partners who need a simple way to work inside the platform.

Portal functions include:

• Submit assignments
• Track open jobs
• Upload photos and notes
• Access billing workflow
• Operate within the Peak Pros platform structure

Platform Structure

Peak Pros USA

National platform layer responsible for billing, payment processing, contractor network infrastructure, and system oversight.

Peak Pros Georgia

State-level operational hub for contractor onboarding, assignment intake, and regional workflow routing.

Territory Operators

Authorized Peak 301 application rights holders who fulfill work inside their designated service areas.

Contractor Partners

Approved roofing and exterior contractors who use the platform to participate in rejuvenation, maintenance, and repair opportunities.
